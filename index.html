Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Instructions

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Heat a frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, in the frying pan.
  4. Add cheese on top of the bread.
  5. Cover with the second slice of bread, butter-side up.
  6. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes, or until golden brown.
  7. Remove from pan and let cool for a minute before serving.
